About CYberAware

CYberAware is a structured cybersecurity awareness and attack simulation platform designed to explain modern cyber threats in a clear, technical, and practical manner. The project was built to bridge the gap between theoretical security concepts and real-world attack scenarios.

The platform classifies major attack categories, maps them to CIA impact, references MITRE ATT&CK techniques, and provides interactive simulations to demonstrate how attacks function and how they can be detected and mitigated.

The goal of this project is to demonstrate applied cybersecurity knowledge, including threat modeling, vulnerability analysis, attack lifecycle understanding, and defensive strategy planning.

This project was independently designed and developed as a portfolio initiative to showcase practical understanding of cybersecurity concepts, structured threat analysis, and secure system awareness.
